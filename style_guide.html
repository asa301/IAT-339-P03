Ethos

I am an illustrator and designer, aspiring to work in game design. I specifically want to focus on player experience, making gameplay captivating and resonate with them. I believe companies looking for a game designer would expect an applicant to be able to ideate and prototype design solutions, understand level design and game mechanics, and be able to collaborate in a team environment. I think I can share two projects that show my qualifications for a job in game design: a video game project, and a user experience design project. Visually and verbally, I am trying to go for a friendly and approachable persona, using the colour blue and having a modern typeface.

Bio

I am an illustrator and designer with a passion for creating games. I care about making meaningful and lasting experiences that engage and inspire.
